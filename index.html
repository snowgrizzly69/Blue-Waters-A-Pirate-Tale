Blue Waters
A Pirate Tale

A Summary of the Voyage

Blue Waters follows the harrowing journey of Edward Jones, a former privateer escaping the gallows during the 1692 Jamaica earthquake. When the disaster swallows Port Royal, he assembles a crew defined by their diverse origins.

Commanding the oak galleon known as The Queen, they flee the Caribbean for the Mediterranean. Throughout the voyage, the crew meets friends and foes shaped by the Siege of Vienna 1683 and the Great Turkish War. They navigate the waters between Venice and the Ottoman Empire, from the ports of Cephalonia to the heights of Methana.
